Our story, problem &
bad news

Everyone loves someone who's had a mental illness. Let’s go deeper. Everyone loves someone who's had a mental illness that led to death by suicide. Yet high levels of stigma persist. This creates a culture of silence, meaning that people will shy away from speaking out and getting the support that they need.

Information on where to get help is also not available. Worse still, there exists myths around mental health further preventing people from seeking help. These myths are fuelled by silence and silence is fuelled by stigma. It’s a never ending loop that needs to be broken!

If this narrative is going to change, someone clearly needs to take action.

What is Artheal
all about?

We envision a society that is mentally whole. We aim to employ a holistic approach in creating mental awareness through art and in facilitating timely access to quality care through strategic partnerships

Ours is not a one-size-fits-all approach. It’s a safe haven of hope & healing where we understand that that every story is unique. Through our holistic approach, we make sure you get the support that you need
