What is Artheal
all about?

We envision a society that is mentally whole. We aim to employ a holistic approach in creating mental awareness through art and in facilitating timely access to quality care through strategic partnerships

Ours is not a one-size-fits-all approach. It’s a safe haven of hope & healing where we understand that that every story is unique. Through our holistic approach, we make sure you get the support that you need

- We create awareness on mental health
- We give you a voice
- We facilitate timely access to quality care
- We destigmatize mental illness
- We equip and empower you
- We give you opportunities to encounter God
